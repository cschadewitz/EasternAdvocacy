Who Are We?

Eastern Advocates is a coalition of EWU alumni, students, faculty and concerned citizens working to ensure that higher education is a priority in Washington. Please, join us in our efforts to support Eastern Washington University.

Supporters of Eastern Advocates were very active the 2013-2014 legislative session.

To find your legislator, you can visit the Washington State Legislature page here. You can also find members on the Higher Education Joint Comittee here.
